making tea

  1. Fill up the kettle with water.
  1. Boil the kettle.
  1. Place a teabag in your favourite mug.
  2. Pour boiling water into your favourite mug.
  3. Brew the tea for a few moments.
  4. Remove and dispose of the teabag.
  5. Add milk.
  6. Add sugar.
